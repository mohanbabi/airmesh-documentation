Airmesh Documentation

Airmesh enables drone control over cellular networks and the internet. This guide outlines how to set up Airmesh, configure the VPN with Tailscale, and establish connections for flight and ground control stations.

Prerequisites

Tailscale Account: Register on Tailscale from your PC or laptop, obtain an authorization (pre-auth) key, and save it securely.

Note: The auth key is shown only once, so store it safely.

Initial Airmesh Setup

Step 1: Physical Setup

  • Follow the circuit diagram to connect the components.
  • Turn on your laptop/PC and power up the circuit with a 5-volt power supply through the servo cable.

Step 2: Connect to Airmesh Network

  • Wait for the Airmesh Wi-Fi hotspot to appear.
  • Connect to the Airmesh network.

Step 3: Access Tailscale Interface

  • Open a web browser and go to http://10.0.2.100:5500.
  • You will see the Tailscale User Interface (UI).

VPN Configuration with Tailscale

Step 1: Connect to Tailscale

  • Click the "Connect to Tailscale" button in the Tailscale UI.
  • When prompted, enter your saved pre-auth key in the "Enter your pre-Auth Key" box.
  • Click the activation button below the input box.

Step 2: Verify VPN Connection

  • Check for a success message confirming the connection.
  • In Tailscale Admin, confirm that the new device has been added. It is now connected to the Tailscale VPN and can communicate with other devices on the network.

Note: If you experience issues during VPN setup, please contact our support team.

Step 3: VPN Setup Reference Video

A full walkthrough is available in our reference video for visual guidance.

Flight Control Configuration

Step 1: Connect to Airmesh Wi-Fi

  • Ensure your device is connected to the Airmesh Wi-Fi network.

Step 2: Access Flight Control Interface

  • In your browser, navigate to http://10.0.2.100:3000.

Step 3: Configure Flight Controller

  • Click "Flight Controllers" on the left side of the page.
  • Follow the video reference instructions to complete the setup.

Ground Control Station Setup

Step 1: Join Tailscale Network

  • Ensure that your Ground Control Station PC or mobile device is connected to the Tailscale network.

Step 2: Control Drone using Internet Service

  • Follow the video reference provided for controlling the drone over the cellular network.
